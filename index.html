I'm the Map is a data visualization application of the different countries of the world

- Start by selecting one of the 6 available continents on the world map
- Next you will be taken to your chosen continent, select one of the many countries to display information about the country
- Finally you can choose to select another country on the current continent or navigate back to the world map by clicking the back button
